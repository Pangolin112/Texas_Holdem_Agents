How to play

  • Fold / Check / Call — the middle button knows whether you can check for free or must call.
  • Raise — opens a slider; drag it or use the ½·¾·pot shortcuts, then Confirm.
  • All-in — shove your whole stack.
  • Say — talk to the table any time. Name someone and they answer; ask why they made a move and they'll explain their real reasoning.
  • Voice — click 🎤 and speak: "fold", "call", "raise to 200", "all in" play the move; anything else is table talk. 🔊 lets the agents talk back out loud.
  • Between hands — buy more chips (added to your tab) or deal the next hand.
  • Autopilot — commit to a move before the turn reaches you: fold in advance, call everything, or call just this street. It clears when the hand ends.
  • Your hand — the panel on the right tracks your best five cards live, and how often each hand you can still make actually wins. When the hand ends you get every seat's cards, strongest first.

A player can bluff about their cards, but if they announce a move ("I fold", "I'm all in") the game forces the move to match.
